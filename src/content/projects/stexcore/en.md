## ✨ What it is

Stexcore Technology is a development team working with international clients. We don't pass you from hand to hand: we design, build and launch your product end to end, and stay by your side after launch.

This site, **stexcore.net**, is our business card: the place where we show what we do and how we work.

The core idea is simple: we take your idea and turn it into real software, using modern, proven tools chosen with care for each project.

## 🚀 Services

- **Mobile apps (Flutter · iOS & Android)** — A single app, smooth on iPhone and Android, ready to publish on the App Store and Google Play, with notifications, camera, maps and biometrics when needed.
- **Web sites & platforms (Next.js)** — From your professional website to custom dashboards to run your business: fast, responsive and ready to scale.
- **Custom systems (NestJS · PostgreSQL)** — The backend that connects your app and web under one system, able to handle thousands of users without slowing down.
- **Online payments** — Integrations with Stripe, PayPal and MercadoPago.
- **Integrations** — We connect your product with other tools and external services.
- **Automated alerts** — Email, SMS and push notifications.
- **Reports & metrics** — Dashboards to see how your business is doing.
- **Maintenance & support** — We stay with you after launch.
